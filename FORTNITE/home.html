FORTNITE

Fortnite is an online video game and game platform developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game in which up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing has been one of the most popular games of recent times.

Epic was able to prepare to release Fortnite as a paid early access title in July 2017, with plans to release it as free-to-play sometime in 2019 while gaining feedback from players to improve the game.
